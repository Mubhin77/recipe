How long do I cook them? The trick to cooking a good chicken burger is watching the cook time
These burgers can go from juicy to bone dry quickly once overcooked. They should take about
4 to 5 minutes per side, but to ensure doneness, use an instant-read meat thermometer:
check that the center of the burger reaches 165ºF, which is the USDA's recommended temperature for poultry.
